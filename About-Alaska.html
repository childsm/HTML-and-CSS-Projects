ALASKA

"The Last Frontier" & "The Land of the Midnight Sun"

John Muir, the famous naturalist, wrote in his journal that you should never go to Alaska as a young man because you'll never be satisfied with any other place as long as you live. And there's a lot of thruth to that. ~Tom Bodett

Alaska was nicknamed The Last Frontier because of its rugged landscape, vast uncharted areas, and distance from the lower 48 states.

Alaska has another nickname, Land of the Midnight Sun due to many areas where the sun is still up at midnight at summer soltice.
In Barrow Alaska during the summer the sun will not set for more than 80 days!
